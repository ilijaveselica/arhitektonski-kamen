Stone Profile

Decorative limestone with architectural positioning

The portfolio includes decorative limestone profiles associated with Rosso-type and Giallo-type commercial positioning, together with technical construction stone applications that support wider resource utilization.

Rosso-type limestone

Decorative pink-red to ochre limestone suitable for high-value architectural use, interior cladding, selected exterior applications, and design-led natural stone projects.

Giallo-type limestone

Yellow-brown decorative limestone suited to vertical and horizontal surfaces in both interior and exterior settings, with broader architectural and construction market positioning.

Technical construction stone

Additional monetization potential through use in asphalt mixes, concrete, road base layers, aggregate applications, and other construction-related uses.
